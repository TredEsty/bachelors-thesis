Description

Titanic is an epic romance and disaster film set during the maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic in 1912. It tells the fictional story of Jack Dawson, a poor artist, and Rose DeWitt Bukater, an aristocratic passenger, who fall in love despite their different social classes. Their passionate relationship unfolds as the unsinkable ship tragically collides with an iceberg and sinks, leading to a desperate struggle for survival.
